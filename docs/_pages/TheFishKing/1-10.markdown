<h3> Author's Notes:</h3>
In a lot of ways, this was an exploratory strip for me. I tried a lot of different things in this one: 1) inking 95% of it by hand and only doing minimal digital post processing, 2) not using any panel borders/gutters it completely a collage of images, and 3) trying to jam in a lot of action without drawing in every little detail.
